Banaras


Varanasi is one of the world's oldest living cities, revered as India’s spiritual capital where pilgrims seek liberation at the sacred Ganges River. It is globally famous for its ancient Kashi Vishwanath Temple, the mesmerizing evening Ganga Aarti, and its profound cultural heritage in silk weaving and music.
